philosophy

Not everything that can be measured is worth showing

Counters and "read" statuses emerged as a technical possibility, not as something a conversation needs. They create a sense of obligation to reply right now. Сalmness deliberately hides what doesn't help communication — and leaves the pace of correspondence up to the person, not the interface.

What's inside

01

Calm presence

Instead of "typing…" and checkmarks — "will reply later" or "online". Fewer signals demanding an immediate reaction.

02

Ephemeral & pinned

Messages are ephemeral by default, like a real conversation. Important ones can be pinned with a single tap and stay.

03

Commands over clicks

The "/" symbol opens a palette: remind, mute, find a message — no menu hunting.

04

Digest instead of feed

Missed 200 messages in a group? See a short topic-based summary instead of endless scrolling.
